# Background Images for DesktopAuth

Place the following 5 images in this directory:

1. **eiffel-sunny.jpg** - Eiffel Tower and Seine River on a sunny day
2. **eiffel-sunset.jpg** - Eiffel Tower at sunrise/sunset
3. **kirkjufell-waterfall.jpg** - Kirkjufell mountain and waterfall at sunset
4. **photographer-sunset.jpg** - Photographer silhouette against sunset sky
5. **boulder-sunset.jpg** - Man pushing boulder up hill at sunset

The images will be automatically blended together in the DesktopAuth component background with various blend modes, opacity, and positioning to create a beautiful layered effect.
